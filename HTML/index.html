About Our ROYAL MOUNT

ROYAL MOUNT a 5 star deluxe Hotel is situated on Karachi’s Abdullah Haroon Rd and is centrally located close to Karachi’s main business and leisure areas including Saddar. Royal Mount is an ideal location for both leisure and business travelers. The Hotel is situated only 10 minutes away from the Karachi International Financial Centre and also a 20 minutes’ drive from Karachi International Airport. ROyal Mount also offers an array of elegant international restaurants and pubs, that are a culinary delight.
